Computer Science

Lately, this term has been thrown around a lot, often when a different term should be used.

com·put·er sci·ence noun a branch of science that deals with the theory of computation or the design of computers.

Basically, designing and creating computer algorithms to be more efficient.

Requirements

Nearly all jobs within the computer sceince field require at least a 4-year (university) bachelor's degree in computer. You can obtain this at most Canadian universities. They have an application process that involves some combination of your high school marks and extra-curriculars (i.e. competitions, volunteering, jobs). Here are some links for more information:
