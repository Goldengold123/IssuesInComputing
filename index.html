General Information

According to Google (definition provided by Oxford Languages):

com·put·ing noun the use or operation of computers.

This means that careers in computing refers to jobs that involve using computers.[1] But this doesn't imply that everything related to computers is a career in computing. For instance, even though an accountant would use technology and computers, it is not a career in computing.

As for what a career in computing actually is, there are three major fields: computer science, computer engineering, and information technology.
